# How to Get Your Portfolio Indexed by Google

Getting your website to appear in Google search results takes time and proper setup. Follow these steps to improve your chances of being found when someone searches "Wassim Dallali".

## Step 1: Verify Your Site is Live and Accessible

1. Visit your site: https://wassim-dallali.netlify.app
2. Make sure it loads correctly
3. Check that all pages are accessible

## Step 2: Submit to Google Search Console

This is the **MOST IMPORTANT** step:

1. **Go to Google Search Console**: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. **Sign in** with your Google account
3. **Add Property**:
   - Click "Add Property"
   - Choose "URL prefix"
   - Enter: `https://wassim-dallali.netlify.app`
4. **Verify Ownership**:
   - Google will give you verification options
   - Choose the HTML tag method (easiest)
   - Copy the verification code
   - Add it to your `index.html` in the `<head>` section
   - Redeploy your site
   - Click "Verify" in Search Console
5. **Submit Sitemap**:
   - Go to "Sitemaps" in the left menu
   - Enter: `https://wassim-dallali.netlify.app/sitemap.xml`
   - Click "Submit"

## Step 3: Request Indexing

1. In Google Search Console, use the **URL Inspection** tool
2. Enter your homepage URL: `https://wassim-dallali.netlify.app`
3. Click "Request Indexing"
4. This tells Google to crawl your site immediately (instead of waiting)

## Step 4: Build Backlinks

The more places that link to your site, the better:

1. **LinkedIn**: Add your portfolio URL to your LinkedIn profile
2. **GitHub**: Add it to your GitHub profile README
3. **Resume**: Include the URL on your resume
4. **Email Signature**: Add it to your email signature
5. **Social Media**: Share your portfolio on Twitter, Facebook, etc.

## Step 5: Create Content with Your Name

1. **Blog Posts**: Write articles mentioning "Wassim Dallali"
2. **GitHub Projects**: Create repositories with your name
3. **Professional Profiles**: Ensure your name is consistent across:
   - LinkedIn
   - GitHub
   - Stack Overflow
   - Medium/Dev.to (if you write articles)

## Step 6: Be Patient

- Google indexing can take **3-7 days** for new sites
- Sometimes it takes **2-4 weeks**
- After submitting to Search Console, check back in a few days

## Step 7: Monitor Progress

1. Check Google Search Console regularly
2. Look for:
   - Coverage reports (pages indexed)
   - Search performance (how often you appear)
   - Any errors or issues

## Step 8: Test Your Search

After a few days, try these searches:
- `site:wassim-dallali.netlify.app` (shows all indexed pages)
- `"Wassim Dallali"` (exact name search)
- `Wassim Dallali developer` (with keywords)

## Additional Tips

3. **Share on Social Media**: Social signals can help
4. **Get Featured**: If you're mentioned in articles or interviews, that helps

## Troubleshooting

**If your site still doesn't appear after 2 weeks:**

1. Check Google Search Console for errors
2. Verify robots.txt allows crawling
3. Ensure your site is mobile-friendly
4. Check page speed (use PageSpeed Insights)
5. Make sure your content is unique and valuable
